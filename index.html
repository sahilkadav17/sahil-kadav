I’m Sahil Kadav,

UI UX Designer

I am a UI/UX Designer with a BSc in Computer Science, who believes that design is not just about creating screens, but about creating understanding. For me, UI/UX is more than colors, buttons, or layouts it’s about the user’s experience, saving their time, and making things easier for them. In every project, I put myself in the user’s place and think from their perspective, which helps me create simple, clean, and meaningful designs. Design keeps evolving, and so do I because I truly believe that good design is not just seen, it is felt.

About Me

I’m Sahil Kadav,

I am a Fashion Communication student with a strong interest in visual storytelling and brand communication.

I am a creative and detail-oriented UI/UX Designer with a BSc in Computer Science. My focus is always on building websites and apps that are not only visually appealing but also easy to use and practical. I understand user needs and design clean layouts, clear user flows, and practical interfaces. Using tools like Figma, UI screens, user flows, and basic web knowledge, I turn ideas into real, usable products. Learning new things, improving design quality, and delivering a better user experience is the way I work.
